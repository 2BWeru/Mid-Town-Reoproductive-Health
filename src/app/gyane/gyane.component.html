Gyna/Obs Ultrasounds Services

An ultrasound helps you find out whether the fetus is developing normally.


Your doctor might recommend that you have one or more ultrasounds to confirm the gestation of the pregnancy or at different points in your pregnancy to check the anatomy of the fetus for defects or problems.
